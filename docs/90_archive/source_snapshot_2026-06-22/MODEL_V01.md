# Model V0.1 - Historical Baseline Record

_Status: historical record. Do not use as the current project-status document._

## Purpose

This document preserves the accepted v0.1 model baseline before Prediction Intelligence v2 work.

## Accepted baseline concepts

- expected-goals model;
- Poisson score matrix;
- 1X2, BTTS, and O/U 2.5 outputs;
- SIGNAL04 and DRAW01 retained at the PR #94 closeout;
- stored pre-match predictions are immutable historical evidence.

## Fair stored evaluation at closeout

- exact score: 7/28;
- BTTS: 16/27;
- O/U 2.5: 16/28;

Current-signal recomputation after a match is diagnostic, not a fair backtest.

## Historical limitations

- thin/partly static signal layer;
- limited durable historical/rating database;
- weak current-tournament responsiveness;
- exact-score-led presentation could look prophetic;
- limited evidence/provenance in premium UX.

## Current relationship

Prediction Intelligence v2 starts from a v1-compatible baseline and applies bounded high-confidence residuals plus a richer scenario/evidence layer. It has not proven a material accuracy breakthrough. See `PREDICTION_INTELLIGENCE_V2_CURRENT.md` for current truth.
